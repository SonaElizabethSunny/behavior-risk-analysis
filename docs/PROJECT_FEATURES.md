# Key Features of "Sentinel AI"

## 1. 🧠 Smart Violence Detection
It doesn't just record video; it understands it. The system can instantly spot fights, punching, kicking, or aggressive behavior in real-time.

## 2. 🔊 Automatic Voice Deterrents
When violence is detected, the computer speaks! It plays a loud warning ("Fight Detected!") to scare off attackers and stop the incident immediately.

## 3. 🎯 Accuracy Check (Anti-False Alarm)
It's smart enough to know the difference between a high-five and a punch. It watches for 2 seconds to confirm a fight is real before raising the alarm.

## 4. 🎥 Intelligent Recording
It doesn't waste space recording empty rooms. It only hits "Record" when violence happens, capturing a focused 5-second video clip of the incident.

## 5. 📧 Email Evidence
It instantly sends an email to security or police with the video clip attached. You get the evidence in your inbox seconds after it happens.

## 6. 📱 SMS Alerts
It sends a text message to your phone immediately, ensuring you know about an emergency even if you aren't at your desk.

## 7. 📍 GPS Location Tracking
Every alert includes the exact GPS location, so responders know exactly where to send help.

## 8. 💻 Live Security Dashboard
A central command center screen that shows live video, a list of all recent alerts, risk levels, and evidence clips in one place.

## 9. 📈 Privacy-First Design
It ignores normal behavior (walking, talking) and only flags danger, respecting privacy until a threat is detected.
