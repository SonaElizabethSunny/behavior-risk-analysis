# How "Sentinel AI" Works: A Simple Explanation

Imagine you have a **digital security guard** who never sleeps, never blinks, and can watch a camera feed 24/7 without getting tired. That is what this project is.

It is an automated surveillance system that uses **Artificial Intelligence (AI)** to proactively stop violence, rather than just recording it.

## The Workflow: Step-by-Step

Here is exactly what happens when the system is running, using a "Fight" scenario:

### 1. The Watchful Eye (Surveillance) 👁️
The system connects to a camera (like a CCTV or webcam) and watches the video feed in real-time. It doesn't just record video; it "looks" at individual frames of video just like a human would.

### 2. The Brain (AI Analysis) 🧠
As it watches, it asks two questions every split second:
*   "Are there humans in this frame?"
*   "Are they fighting or hurting each other?"

It acts as a filter. If people are just walking or talking (Normal), it does nothing. It stays quiet.

### 3. The Incident (Detection & Verification) ⚠️
Suddenly, two people start fighting.
*   The system notices the violent movement immediately.
*   **The Smart Check:** To avoid false alarms (like someone just waving their hands), it waits for **2 seconds**. It wants to be sure this is real violence, not just a misunderstanding.

### 4. The Immediate Response (The Voice) 🔊
Once it confirms the fight is real (after 2 seconds):
*   **It Speaks Up:** The computer immediately plays a loud voice alert: *"Fight or assault detected!"*
*   **Goal:** This acts as a deterrent to stop the attackers *while* the crime is happening, not hours later.

### 5. Collecting Evidence (The Witness) 🎥
At the exact moment it plays the alarm, it hits the **"Record" button**.
*   It captures a **5-second video clip** of the incident.
*   It ensures we have clear proof of what happened, without filling up hard drives with hours of empty footage.

### 6. Calling for Help (The Messenger) 🚑
While the smart guard deals with the local threat, it simultaneously alerts the human authorities:
*   **The Dashboard:** A detailed alert pops up on the security computer screen with the video clip attached.
*   **Email:** It sends an email with the video evidence to the police or admin.
*   **SMS:** It sends a text message to a designated phone number with the GPS location of the fight.

## In Summary

**Without Sentinel AI:**
A fight happens → Camera records it → Security guard is on break/sleeping → Victim gets hurt → Hours later, someone reviews footage → Too late.

**With Sentinel AI:**
A fight happens → AI sees it → **AI shouts "STOP"** → Sends video to phone immediately → Help arrives fast + Evidence is saved.
